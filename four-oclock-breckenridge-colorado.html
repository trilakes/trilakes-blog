About Four OClock

Located just a stone's throw from the bustling town of Breckenridge, the Four OClock neighborhood is a hidden gem nestled in the stunning Rocky Mountains. This area is known for its beautiful views of the surrounding peaks, along with its proximity to world-class skiing, hiking, and outdoor recreational opportunities. The neighborhood boasts a mix of single-family homes, townhouses, and condominiums, making it appealing to a variety of buyers, from families to retirees and investors. The tranquil atmosphere, combined with easy access to downtown Breckenridge and local amenities, enhances the neighborhood's allure. For outdoor enthusiasts, the nearby hiking and biking trails, as well as the Four O'Clock Ski Run, offer year-round adventure. With its blend of scenic beauty and community spirit, Four OClock is an ideal location for those looking to build or buy a home in a vibrant mountain setting.

Water & Utilities

The Four OClock neighborhood is serviced by municipal water, which is a significant advantage for residents. This means homeowners do not have to rely on wells or cisterns, providing a consistent and reliable water supply. The infrastructure is well-maintained, ensuring that residents have access to clean, potable water year-round. The municipal system is generally robust, but it's always wise to check with the local water authority for any updates or potential changes to service. If you are considering building a custom home, it's essential to understand the water lines and connections specific to your property. For those interested in well water, you can research the feasibility and regulations at ColoradoWell.com. However, for the majority of residents in Four OClock, municipal water offers peace of mind and convenience.
