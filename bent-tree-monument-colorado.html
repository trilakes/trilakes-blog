About Bent Tree

Bent Tree is a picturesque neighborhood located just south of Monument, Colorado, nestled against the stunning backdrop of the Rocky Mountains. Known for its serene atmosphere and natural beauty, Bent Tree offers residents a unique blend of suburban convenience and rural charm. The area's scenic views, abundant wildlife, and proximity to outdoor recreational activities make it an attractive destination for families, retirees, and nature enthusiasts alike. The community is characterized by its spacious lots, many of which feature mature trees and custom-built homes that reflect the local architectural style. Property types commonly found in Bent Tree range from single-family residences on larger lots to newer constructions that cater to diverse tastes. The neighborhood's appeal lies not only in its tranquil setting but also in its strong sense of community, making it an ideal place to call home.

Water & Utilities

The Bent Tree neighborhood primarily relies on individual wells for water supply, as municipal water services are not available in this area. Residents can expect a robust well water infrastructure, with most properties equipped with adequately maintained wells. Homebuyers should be aware of the importance of water quality and quantity assessments, as these factors can significantly impact property value and livability. For more detailed information regarding well regulations and best practices, prospective buyers can consult ColoradoWell.com. It's advisable to conduct thorough research and seek professional guidance when evaluating well water systems, including the potential need for filtration or treatment systems to ensure safe drinking water. Overall, the availability of well water in Bent Tree allows residents to enjoy the benefits of living in a natural setting while accommodating their water needs.
