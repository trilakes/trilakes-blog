About Aspen Highlands

Aspen Highlands is a picturesque neighborhood located just a few miles from the charming town of Aspen, Colorado. Known for its stunning mountain views and access to world-class skiing, Aspen Highlands boasts a unique blend of natural beauty and luxury living. The area is particularly appealing to outdoor enthusiasts, with an abundance of hiking, biking, and skiing opportunities right at your doorstep. The community is characterized by upscale single-family homes, townhomes, and condominiums, many of which offer breathtaking views of the surrounding peaks. Additionally, the proximity to the Aspen Highlands Ski Resort makes it an attractive option for those looking to enjoy the slopes without straying too far from home. With a vibrant community atmosphere, excellent schools, and convenient access to amenities, Aspen Highlands is a desirable location for both permanent residents and second homeowners alike.
