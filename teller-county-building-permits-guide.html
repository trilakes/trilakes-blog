Building in Teller County, Colorado, offers a unique blend of scenic mountain views and a tight-knit community atmosphere. Building in Teller County requires diligence and patience, but with the right support, you can successfully navigate the process.

Septic System Permits (OWTS)

In Teller County, septic system permits, also known as Onsite Wastewater Treatment Systems (OWTS), are separate from building permits and are regulated by the County Health Department. If your property is not connected to a municipal sewer system, obtaining a septic permit is crucial before beginning construction. The process starts with a soil test and a site evaluation to determine the suitability of your land for a septic system. Common Permit Delays & How to Avoid

Understanding common permit delays in Teller County can save you time and frustration. One of the most frequent causes of delays is missing documentation.
