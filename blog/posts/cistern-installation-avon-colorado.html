Why Avon Properties Need Professional Cistern Installation

In the stunning mountain community of Avon, Colorado, where the elevation reaches over 8,000 feet, reliable access to water is essential for homes and properties. Many properties here do not have the luxury of a well due to the challenging terrain and deep water tables, making cistern installation a vital solution for homeowners. A professionally installed underground water storage tank not only ensures a consistent supply of potable water but also enhances property value and provides peace of mind. At Tri-Lakes Contracting, our licensed and insured team understands the unique needs of Avon residents, ensuring that your water storage tank installation meets all local codes and environmental considerations.

Our Cistern Installation Process

At Tri-Lakes Contracting, we follow a thorough process for cistern installation to ensure the best results for your property. The installation phase generally spans 2-3 days, depending on site conditions. Our skilled team will excavate the area, prepare the site, and install the tank while ensuring it is properly insulated for winter. Cost Factors for Cistern Installation in Avon

Understanding the costs associated with water storage tank installation in Avon is essential for budgeting your project. For a 5,000 gallon Norwesco tank, you can expect to pay between $13,000 and $15,000, while a 10,000 gallon tank typically ranges from $22,000 to $28,000. However, the total installed cost often exceeds these tank prices due to several factors including excavation, equipment, and additional components.

Excavation can add between $3,000 and $8,000+, particularly in rocky terrain, which is common in Avon. You should also factor in costs for pump systems and pressure tanks ($2,500 to $5,000), trenching ($25 to $50 per linear foot), and various electrical and access components. A complete installation for a basic 5,000 gallon system can range from $25,000 to $35,000, while a 10,000 gallon system can run between $40,000 and $55,000, depending on site conditions and specific needs. Remember, the size of your cistern should be based on your daily water usage (bedroom count x 2 people x 100 gallons/day) and how often you want to refill it, which can significantly affect overall costs.

Frequently Asked Questions About Cistern Installation

Q: How much does a cistern cost in Colorado?

A: The cost of a cistern in Colorado can vary significantly based on size and site conditions. For example, a 5,000 gallon tank typically costs between $25,000 and $35,000, while a larger 10,000 gallon tank installation can range from $40,000 to $55,000.

Q: What size underground water storage tank do I need?

A: The size of your underground water storage tank should be determined based on your daily water usage. For instance, a 3-bedroom home will generally require a minimum of 600 gallons per day, suggesting a tank size of at least 18,000 gallons for monthly refills or even larger for less frequent refills.

Q: How long does cistern installation take?

A: Cistern installation typically takes 2-3 days, depending on site conditions such as soil type and access challenges. Our team will conduct a thorough site assessment to provide a more accurate timeline before starting the project.

Q: Cistern vs well - which is better?

A: The choice between a cistern and a well depends on various factors, including water table depth and property location. Cisterns are often a better option in areas with deep water tables or rocky terrain, like many parts of Avon, as they provide a reliable water source without the complications of drilling a well.
