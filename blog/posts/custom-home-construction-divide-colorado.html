Our Custom Home Construction Process

At Tri-Lakes Contracting, we follow a comprehensive step-by-step process for custom home construction that ensures every detail is addressed, from initial conception to the final walk-through:

  • Step 1: Pre-Purchase Build Feasibility Study - We start with a thorough evaluation of the land to assess whether it is buildable, looking at soil conditions, water flow, and setback requirements.
  • Step 2: Well Permit - Securing a water source is crucial. We advise filing your well permit application early to avoid delays.
  • Step 3: Site Plan - Our professionals will design a site plan that optimally positions your home, driveway, and utility lines.
  • Step 4: Architectural Plans - Whether you bring your own architect or choose from our network, we will help you create the perfect design for your mountain home.
  • Step 5: Engineered Foundation & Framing Plans - These plans are essential for ensuring your home can withstand local conditions.
  • Step 6: Land Survey - A land survey helps in properly verifying property boundaries and easements.
  • Step 7: Septic System Plans - We will design an engineered septic system that meets all local regulations.
  • Step 8: Compile & Submit for Building Permit - We handle all the paperwork and submissions for your building permit through the Teller County Building Department.
  • Step 9: Pre-Construction Planning - We finalize all plans, budgets, and schedules before breaking ground.
  • Step 10: Construction - Our team manages the entire construction process, ensuring quality and compliance with inspections.
  • Step 11: Certificate of Occupancy & Move In! - After final inspections, you receive your certificate of occupancy, and it's time to move into your new custom home!

Cost Factors for Custom Home Construction in Divide

Understanding the costs associated with custom home building in Divide is essential for budgeting your project effectively. The cost per square foot generally falls within these ranges:

  • Basic build: $350-$450/sq ft
  • Standard quality: $450-$550/sq ft
  • Custom/higher-end: $550-$700/sq ft
  • Luxury/high-end: $700-$1,000+/sq ft

For example, a 2,000 sq ft home could cost between $700,000 and $1,100,000 depending on the quality of finishes and complexity of the build. Additional costs, such as land acquisition, septic installation ($35,000-$80,000), and driveway construction ($10,000-$50,000), should also be factored into your overall budget.

If you opt for a cistern for your water supply, consider that sizing will typically be based on the number of bedrooms in your home, calculated as 2 people per bedroom x 100 gallons per day. For instance, a three-bedroom home would require a cistern of at least 6,000 gallons to ensure adequate supply.

Frequently Asked Questions About Custom Home Construction

Q: How much does it cost to build a custom home in Colorado?

A: The cost to build a custom home in Colorado varies widely, but typically ranges from $350 to $1,000+ per square foot depending on design complexity and quality of materials. For a 2,500 sq ft home, costs can range between $875,000 and $1,375,000.

Q: How long does it take to build a house?

A: The typical timeline for building a custom home in Colorado is about 10 to 18 months, depending on factors such as weather conditions and the complexity of the build. Pre-construction planning can take an additional 2 to 6 months.

Q: What should I look for in a custom home builder?

A: When selecting a custom home builder, consider their experience with mountain construction, understanding of local building codes, and ability to manage the unique challenges of your site. Look for a licensed and insured general contractor with positive client testimonials.

Q: Should I get a feasibility study before buying land?

A: Yes, obtaining a pre-purchase build feasibility report is essential to determine if the land is suitable for your intended construction. This study assesses soil conditions, water flow, and local regulations, helping you avoid costly mistakes.
