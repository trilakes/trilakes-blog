Frequently Asked Questions About Excavation Services

Q: How much does excavation cost per cubic yard?

A: Excavation costs can vary widely based on site conditions, but generally, you can expect rates starting from $8 to $15 per cubic yard for standard excavation. For rocky conditions, this can increase to $20 to $50 per cubic yard.

Q: How long does site preparation take?

A: The timeline for site preparation typically ranges from a few days to several weeks, depending on the complexity of the project and the specific challenges of the site. Factors like weather conditions and accessibility also play a significant role in determining the overall timeline.

Q: What equipment is needed for mountain excavation?

A: Mountain excavation requires specialized equipment to handle rocky and uneven terrain. Common equipment includes excavators, bobcats for smaller tasks, and dump trucks for material removal. Each piece of equipment is essential for efficiently navigating the unique challenges of Franktown's landscape.

Q: Do I need permits for excavation?

A: Yes, excavation projects in Franktown require permits from the Douglas County building department. It's essential to secure these permits before starting any work to ensure compliance with local regulations and avoid potential fines.
