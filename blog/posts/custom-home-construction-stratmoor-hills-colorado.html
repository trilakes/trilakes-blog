Why Stratmoor Hills Properties Need Professional Custom Home Construction

Stratmoor Hills, situated at an elevation of over 8,000 feet, presents a stunning backdrop for future homeowners, but it also comes with its own unique set of challenges. For those looking to create a custom home in this mountainous community, partnering with a licensed and insured general contractor like Tri-Lakes Contracting is essential. Our extensive knowledge of the region ensures that every aspect of your home construction project is handled with expertise, from navigating building codes to understanding local soil conditions. This expertise is especially crucial in Stratmoor Hills, where steep driveways and rocky terrains can complicate even the simplest of builds.

Local Challenges in El Paso County

Building in El Paso County, particularly in Stratmoor Hills, requires a thorough understanding of the local environment. The rocky granite soil and varying clay types in valleys can lead to significant challenges, especially when it comes to foundation engineering and site planning. Properties often require engineered septic systems due to difficult soil conditions, and the water table can be quite deep, necessitating cisterns or water hauling solutions.

The elevation also plays a significant role; at 8,000 feet, construction must adhere to deeper frost lines (36-48 inches), which can increase costs and complexity. Furthermore, access to many properties can be limited during winter months, making planning for construction timelines vital. Homeowners must also consider fire mitigation strategies in forested areas, ensuring defensible space around their new mountain home. With our experience, we can help you navigate these local challenges effectively.

Our Custom Home Construction Process

At Tri-Lakes Contracting, we follow a meticulous step-by-step process to ensure that your custom build is completed to your satisfaction. Our journey begins with a pre-purchase property inspection and a detailed build feasibility report to assess whether your desired location is suitable for construction. If you proceed, we move into the well permit stage, crucial for securing water access.

Next, we develop a comprehensive site plan that details the positioning of your home, driveway, and essential utilities. Following this, we work on architectural plans, which can either be sourced from our network of architects or from ArchitecturalDesigns.com, offering you thousands of ready-made plans tailored for mountain living.

The engineering phase follows, where we ensure that your foundation and structural plans are compliant with Colorado's stringent building codes. After obtaining all necessary building permits from the El Paso County Building Department, we can finally commence construction. Throughout the process, we prioritize your input, ensuring that the final product is not only beautiful but also functional.

Cost Factors for Custom Home Construction in Stratmoor Hills

When considering a custom home builder in Colorado's mountains, it's important to understand the financial landscape. The cost per square foot for a basic build in this area typically ranges from $350 to $450, while standard quality projects may fall between $450 and $550. For custom or higher-end homes, expect to spend between $550 and $700 per square foot, with luxury builds reaching $1,000 or more. For example, a 2,500 square foot custom home could cost around $1,375,000 or more, depending on the specifics.

Additionally, factors such as rocky terrain, steep access, and the need for engineered septic systems can increase costs significantly. If you plan to use a cistern for water, remember the formula for sizing: two people per bedroom at 100 gallons per day. This means a three-bedroom home requires a cistern with a capacity of at least 6,000 gallons to ensure ample water supply. As a general contractor, we help you navigate these costs effectively, ensuring no surprises arise during your home building journey.

Frequently Asked Questions About Custom Home Construction

Q: How much does it cost to build a custom home in Colorado?

A: The cost to build a custom home in Colorado typically ranges from $350 to $1,000+ per square foot, depending on the quality and complexity of the project. For a luxury custom home, you might expect to pay upwards of $1,500,000 for a 2,500 sq ft build.

Q: How long does it take to build a house?

A: The construction timeline can vary significantly; generally, the pre-construction phase takes 2-6 months, while actual building can last 10-18 months. Complex or larger homes may extend this timeline even further.

Q: What should I look for in a custom home builder?

A: When selecting a custom home builder, look for licensed and insured contractors with experience in your desired area. Check their portfolio, client references, and ensure they are familiar with local building codes and site conditions for a successful project.

Q: Should I get a feasibility study before buying land?

A: Yes, obtaining a build feasibility report before purchasing land is crucial. This report assesses site conditions, setback requirements, and overall buildability, helping you avoid potential costly mistakes.
