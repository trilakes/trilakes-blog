About Fall River

The Fall River neighborhood, nestled just outside Idaho Springs, Colorado, offers a unique blend of rustic charm and outdoor adventure. The community is characterized by its cozy cabins, spacious single-family homes, and a few modern builds, making it an appealing choice for both year-round residents and vacationers seeking a mountain retreat. Water & Utilities
