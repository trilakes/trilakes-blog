About Temple Canyon Area

The Temple Canyon Area, nestled just a few miles outside of Canon City, Colorado, is a hidden gem known for its breathtaking natural beauty and a tight-knit community atmosphere. The neighborhood is characterized by its stunning canyon views, mature trees, and proximity to outdoor recreational opportunities. Residents enjoy easy access to hiking, biking, and exploring the scenic Arkansas River, making it ideal for nature lovers and outdoor enthusiasts alike. The area features a mix of properties, from cozy cabins to custom-built homes, often with large lots that provide plenty of space for privacy and outdoor activities. With its serene environment, stunning vistas, and convenient access to city amenities, Temple Canyon is a perfect spot for those looking to escape the hustle and bustle while still being close to the conveniences of Canon City.

Building & Permits

Building in the Temple Canyon Area falls under the jurisdiction of Fremont County, which manages all construction permits and zoning regulations. Before starting a building project, it's crucial to familiarize yourself with the specific requirements set forth by the county.
