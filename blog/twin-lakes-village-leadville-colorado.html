About Twin Lakes Village

Twin Lakes Village is a picturesque neighborhood nestled near Leadville, Colorado, surrounded by breathtaking mountain vistas and the serene waters of Twin Lakes. Known for its tranquil environment and outdoor recreational opportunities, this community is a haven for nature lovers, hikers, and those looking to escape the hustle and bustle of city life. The area boasts a charming mix of rustic cabin-style homes, modern builds, and vacant lots offering a chance for custom construction. With its proximity to the breathtaking Collegiate Peaks and easy access to hiking trails, fishing, and boating, Twin Lakes Village appeals to families, retirees, and weekend adventurers alike. The local community is warm and welcoming, fostering a sense of belonging among residents.

Water & Utilities

In Twin Lakes Village, residents primarily rely on private wells for their water supply, as the area does not have municipal water services. Before purchasing a property, it's crucial to research the well's water quality and depth. Resources such as ColoradoWell.com provide valuable information on well regulations, maintenance, and testing. Additionally, consider the possibility of installing a cistern for backup water storage, especially during the drier months. The local infrastructure for water delivery is generally adequate, but prospective buyers should assess the well's production capability and quality to ensure it meets their household needs. Understanding the water rights and regulations in Colorado will help inform your decision-making process.

Septic Considerations

Twin Lakes Village does not have a centralized sewer system, so most properties utilize septic systems for wastewater management. Common types of septic systems in the area include conventional systems, mound systems, and aerobic treatment units, depending on the specific terrain and soil conditions. The mountainous terrain can present challenges, including sloped lots or rocky soil, which may impact the type of septic system required.
