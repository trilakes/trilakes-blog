About High Park Ranches

High Park Ranches is a stunning, mountainous neighborhood located just northwest of Florissant, Colorado. This picturesque enclave is nestled in a serene setting, surrounded by the majestic Rocky Mountains, which makes it an appealing destination for nature lovers, outdoor enthusiasts, and those seeking a tranquil lifestyle away from the hustle and bustle of city life. The area boasts a mix of spacious lots and charming cabins, with property types ranging from rustic mountain homes to modern retreats, each offering breathtaking views and abundant wildlife. With its close proximity to Pike National Forest, residents can enjoy hiking, fishing, and camping right at their doorstep. High Park Ranches also fosters a strong sense of community, making it an ideal choice for families and individuals looking to build a life in harmony with nature.

Water & Utilities

In High Park Ranches, water is primarily sourced from wells, as the area does not have access to municipal water systems. Homeowners typically rely on private wells or cisterns for their water supply. Septic Considerations

High Park Ranches primarily utilizes septic systems for wastewater management, as the area is not serviced by municipal sewer lines. The most common type of septic system in the region is a conventional septic tank, which is effective in handling household wastewater. However, depending on the specific terrain and soil conditions of your property, alternative systems may be required, such as engineered systems or mound systems. It's crucial to have a thorough soil test conducted before installation to ensure proper drainage and functionality. The mountainous terrain can present unique challenges, including steep slopes and rocky soil, which may affect the septic system design. Be prepared to work with local experts to navigate these considerations and ensure compliance with state and county regulations.
