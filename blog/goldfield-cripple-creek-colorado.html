About Goldfield

The Goldfield neighborhood is a charming and historic area located just a few miles from Cripple Creek, Colorado. Nestled in the foothills of the Rockies, Goldfield is known for its stunning natural beauty, rich mining heritage, and a tight-knit community atmosphere. The neighborhood features a blend of rustic mountain cabins, modern homes, and vacant lots that offer ample opportunities for building your dream residence. Its proximity to Cripple Creek, a popular destination for gaming and entertainment, adds to its appeal, making Goldfield an attractive option for both full-time residents and vacation homeowners. Its serene environment, coupled with the breathtaking views of the surrounding mountains and valleys, sets the stage for a peaceful lifestyle amidst Colorado's wilderness.

Water & Utilities

In the Goldfield area, most properties rely on wells for their water supply, as municipal water services are limited. Homeowners often utilize cisterns for storing water, particularly in more remote locations. If you're considering building or buying, it's crucial to research the water infrastructure in place. Many existing homes in the area have successfully drilled wells, but it's advisable to consult resources like ColoradoWell.com for information about local water wells, water quality, and regulations. Understanding the water rights and the availability of groundwater in this mountainous terrain is essential for planning your property and ensuring a reliable water supply for your household. It's also wise to have a water quality test performed to guarantee the safety and potability of the well water.
