About Wet Mountain Valley

The Wet Mountain Valley, located near the charming town of Westcliffe, Colorado, is a picturesque region known for its stunning landscapes and outdoor recreational opportunities. Nestled in the foothills of the Sangre de Cristo Mountains, the area boasts breathtaking views, abundant wildlife, and a peaceful atmosphere that attracts nature lovers, retirees, and families alike. The valley is appealing for its small-town charm combined with access to outdoor activities such as hiking, fishing, and horseback riding. Property types in Wet Mountain Valley range from spacious parcels of land perfect for custom homes to cozy cabins and manufactured homes, providing diverse options for potential buyers and builders.

Water & Utilities

In Wet Mountain Valley, most properties rely on wells for water supply, as municipal water services are limited. Homeowners typically drill wells to access groundwater, which is generally reliable in this region. However, it is essential to conduct thorough research on well depths and water quality before purchasing land. Resources such as ColoradoWell.com can provide valuable insights into local well data and regulations. Additionally, many properties in the area use cisterns for water storage, particularly if they are located in more remote sections of the valley. When considering building or purchasing in Wet Mountain Valley, it is crucial to factor in the cost of drilling a well, the potential need for a cistern, and the ongoing maintenance of these water systems.

Septic Considerations
