About Intermountain

The Intermountain neighborhood, nestled just a short drive from Vail, Colorado, offers a unique blend of mountain charm and accessibility. This residential area is known for its stunning views of the surrounding peaks and its proximity to world-class skiing, hiking, and outdoor activities. The appeal of Intermountain lies in its serene atmosphere, making it a perfect retreat for both year-round residents and seasonal visitors. Property types in this area vary widely, including single-family homes, townhomes, and condominiums, with many properties featuring rustic designs that complement the natural landscape. The neighborhood's combination of convenience to Vail's vibrant culture and the tranquility of mountain living makes it a highly sought-after location for those looking to buy or build in the region.

Water & Utilities

The Intermountain area primarily relies on municipal water systems, ensuring residents have access to clean and reliable water. For those considering properties with wells, it is essential to research the availability and regulations surrounding well use. Websites like ColoradoWell.com can provide valuable information on water rights, well depths, and the overall groundwater quality in the area. Water infrastructure in Intermountain is generally well-maintained, allowing for efficient water distribution. Residents can expect consistent pressure and quality, but it's wise to inquire about any specific issues related to your prospective property, especially if it's an older home. Additionally, residents should stay informed about potential future developments that may impact water availability or quality.

Building & Permits

The Eagle County Government oversees building permits in the Intermountain area. If you plan to build a new home or undertake significant renovations, you will need to secure the appropriate permits. The county's website provides detailed guidelines and requirements for construction projects, making it easier for prospective builders to navigate the process.
