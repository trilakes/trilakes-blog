How Much Does Cistern Installation Cost in Colorado?

By Kyle Rossignol, Licensed GC
```html

Quick Answer

The cost of cistern installation in Colorado typically ranges from $3,000 to $10,000, depending on various factors such as size, material, location, and specific site conditions. For more complex installations or larger systems, costs can reach up to $15,000 or more.

Detailed Explanation

When considering cistern installation in Colorado, several factors influence the overall cost. Understanding these components can help you budget effectively and avoid unexpected expenses. Here‚Äôs a breakdown of key factors affecting installation costs:

  • Size of the Cistern: The size you choose will directly impact the cost. Common residential cisterns range from 1,500 to 5,000 gallons. Larger systems for commercial use can exceed 10,000 gallons, driving up costs significantly.
  • Material: Cisterns can be made from plastic, fiberglass, or concrete. Plastic cisterns are generally more affordable, while concrete cisterns can be more durable but also more expensive. For example, a 2,500-gallon concrete cistern may cost about $7,000, while a similar plastic option might be around $4,000.
  • Site Conditions: The accessibility of your property and soil conditions can affect labor costs. If the site is difficult to access or requires extensive excavation, you may see an increase in overall costs.
  • Permits and Regulations: In Colorado, local regulations may require permits for cistern installation, which can add to the costs. Make sure to check with local authorities to understand any specific requirements.
  • Additional Features: If you're adding features such as filtration systems, pumps, or monitoring equipment, these will also raise the overall price. For instance, a high-quality filtration system can add an additional $1,000 to $3,000 to your total.
```
