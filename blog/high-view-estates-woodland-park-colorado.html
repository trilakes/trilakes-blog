About High View Estates

High View Estates is a serene and scenic neighborhood nestled just outside of Woodland Park, Colorado, offering residents stunning views of the Rocky Mountains and easy access to outdoor recreational activities. This community is characterized by its spacious lots, many of which are over an acre, providing ample room for privacy and personal development. The natural beauty of the area is complemented by its proximity to national forests, hiking trails, and pristine lakes, making it a haven for nature lovers and outdoor enthusiasts. High View Estates is particularly appealing for those seeking a peaceful rural lifestyle while still being close to the amenities of Woodland Park, which features shops, dining, and community events. The property types in this neighborhood primarily consist of single-family homes, many of which are custom-built, alongside vacant lots ideal for anyone looking to build their dream home.

Water & Utilities

In High View Estates, the water supply primarily comes from individual wells, as municipal water services are limited in this area. Each property typically has its own well or may use a cistern system, depending on the specific needs and preferences of the homeowner. Building & Permits

Building in High View Estates falls under the jurisdiction of Teller County, which oversees all permitting processes for new construction and renovations. Before starting any building project, it is vital to familiarize yourself with local zoning laws, property setbacks, and building codes. Additionally, there may be restrictions imposed by the Homeowners Association (HOA) that governs the community, which can include guidelines on architectural styles, fencing, and landscaping. Understanding these regulations in advance will help streamline the construction process and ensure compliance. It is also advisable to consult with the Teller County Building Department for specific requirements and to obtain all necessary permits before commencing any construction project.
