About Red Mountain

Red Mountain is an exclusive and picturesque neighborhood located just a few miles from the vibrant town of Aspen, Colorado. Known for its stunning views of the Elk Mountains and proximity to world-class ski resorts, this area is a haven for outdoor enthusiasts and those seeking a luxurious mountain lifestyle. The combination of privacy, natural beauty, and easy access to Aspen's amenities makes Red Mountain highly desirable. Property types in this area range from expansive estates and luxurious chalets to custom-built mountain homes, catering to a variety of tastes and preferences. Additionally, the neighborhood boasts a sense of community, with several social events and gatherings that foster connections among residents.
