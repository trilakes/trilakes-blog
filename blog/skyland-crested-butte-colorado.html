About Skyland

Skyland is a picturesque neighborhood nestled just outside Crested Butte, Colorado, offering stunning mountain views, abundant recreational opportunities, and a peaceful atmosphere that attracts both full-time residents and vacationers. The area is known for its proximity to the Crested Butte Ski Resort, making it a prime location for winter sports enthusiasts. In the warmer months, residents enjoy hiking, biking, and fishing in the nearby trails and lakes. The combination of natural beauty and a close-knit community makes Skyland an appealing choice for those looking to build or buy a home in the Rockies.

Common property types in Skyland include single-family homes, luxury chalets, and townhomes, many of which boast modern amenities and stunning views of the surrounding mountains. The real estate market in this area is diverse, catering to various budgets and preferences, from cozy cabins to expansive estates. Additionally, the neighborhood benefits from a friendly, welcoming atmosphere that fosters a sense of belonging and community engagement.

Water & Utilities

Skyland is equipped with a municipal water system, ensuring residents have consistent access to clean water. This is a significant advantage over other rural areas that may rely on wells or cisterns. Septic Considerations

In Skyland, most properties are connected to a municipal sewer system, which simplifies waste management for homeowners. However, if you're exploring more remote properties or older homes, you may encounter septic systems. Common types of septic systems include conventional systems, alternative systems, and engineered systems, depending on soil conditions and property layout.

The terrain in Skyland can vary, with some homes situated on sloped lots, which may require specialized septic installation. It's crucial to have a professional assess the land to determine the best septic solution if needed. Additionally, local regulations regarding septic systems are enforced, so it's wise to familiarize yourself with these requirements before making a purchase or planning a build.

Building & Permits

Crested Butte and the surrounding areas, including Skyland, fall under the jurisdiction of Gunnison County for building permits. If you're considering building a new home or making significant renovations, you'll need to navigate the permitting process, which typically involves submitting plans and obtaining the necessary approvals from county officials.

Additionally, Skyland has a Homeowners Association (HOA) that imposes certain restrictions and design guidelines to maintain the aesthetic and environmental integrity of the neighborhood.
