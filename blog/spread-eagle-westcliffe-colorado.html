About Spread Eagle

The Spread Eagle neighborhood, nestled in the scenic Wet Mountain Valley near Westcliffe, Colorado, offers a unique blend of natural beauty and rural charm. With stunning views of the Sangre de Cristo Mountains and abundant outdoor recreational opportunities, it's an ideal location for those seeking a tranquil lifestyle away from the hustle and bustle of city life. The area is known for its spacious lots, allowing for ample privacy and the chance to enjoy the surrounding wilderness. Properties here typically include single-family homes, cabins, and land parcels suitable for building, making it an attractive option for both new buyers and those looking to invest in vacation rentals. The community is welcoming, with a strong sense of camaraderie among its residents, making it a perfect place for families, retirees, and outdoor enthusiasts alike.

Septic Considerations

The Spread Eagle neighborhood primarily employs septic systems for wastewater management, as there is no centralized sewer system. Most homes utilize conventional septic systems, which consist of a septic tank and a drain field. It's important to assess the soil conditions and terrain of the lot you are considering, as rocky or sloped land can complicate septic installation. A soil test is often required to determine the suitability of the land for a septic system. Additionally, be aware that obtaining a permit for a septic system can take time, so it's advisable to start this process early in your planning. Working with local professionals familiar with septic regulations can help streamline the process and ensure your system meets all health and safety standards.
