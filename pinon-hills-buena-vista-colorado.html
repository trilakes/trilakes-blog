About Pinon Hills

Pinon Hills is a charming neighborhood situated just outside of Buena Vista, Colorado, offering residents stunning views of the Collegiate Peaks and a serene mountain lifestyle. This community is best known for its spacious lots, many of which are adorned with mature ponderosa pines and native vegetation, creating a picturesque backdrop for outdoor living. What makes Pinon Hills particularly appealing is its balance between tranquility and accessibility; residents can easily enjoy the vibrant offerings of Buena Vista while relishing the peace of a more rural setting. The area is characterized by single-family homes, many of which are custom-built, as well as a few townhome developments. With ample opportunities for outdoor activities such as hiking, fishing, and mountain biking, Pinon Hills is a haven for nature enthusiasts and those seeking an active lifestyle.

Septic Considerations

Pinon Hills is not connected to a municipal sewer system; thus, most homes utilize septic systems for waste management. The type of septic system commonly installed in this area varies, but conventional systems are prevalent, as they work well with the terrain. Building & Permits

Building in Pinon Hills falls under the jurisdiction of Chaffee County, which handles all necessary permits for construction. Before you break ground, it is important to familiarize yourself with the local building codes and zoning regulations. Additionally, some areas within Pinon Hills may have Homeowners Association (HOA) restrictions that dictate architectural styles, landscaping, and other community standards. Make sure to review any HOA guidelines to ensure your plans comply. A thorough understanding of the permitting process and local regulations will facilitate a smoother building experience and help avoid potential setbacks.
