About Tin Cup

Tin Cup is a small, picturesque town located in Gunnison County, Colorado, known for its stunning mountain views and rich history. Once a thriving mining settlement, it has transformed into a peaceful haven for nature lovers, outdoor enthusiasts, and those seeking a slower pace of life. With its proximity to the stunning Taylor River and surrounding wilderness, residents enjoy numerous recreational activities such as hiking, fishing, skiing, and camping.
