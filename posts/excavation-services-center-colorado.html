Frequently Asked Questions About Excavation Services

Q: How much does excavation cost per cubic yard?

A: The cost for excavation services typically varies based on site conditions, with average costs ranging from $8 to $15 per cubic yard for standard excavation. However, for rocky conditions, this can rise to $20-$50 per cubic yard, especially in the challenging terrain of Saguache County.

Q: How long does site preparation take?

A: The timeline for site preparation can vary based on the scope of the project and site conditions. Generally, excavation services in our area can take anywhere from a few days to several weeks, particularly if extensive grading or foundation excavation is required.

Q: What equipment is needed for mountain excavation?

A: Effective mountain excavation often requires specialized equipment such as excavators, bobcats, and dump trucks. Tri-Lakes Contracting utilizes state-of-the-art machinery to handle the unique challenges of our rocky and elevated terrain, ensuring precise and efficient work.

Q: Do I need permits for excavation?

A: Yes, excavation projects in Center must go through the Saguache County Building Department for permits. This regulation ensures that all excavation work complies with local laws and safety standards, which is critical for protecting your property and investment.
