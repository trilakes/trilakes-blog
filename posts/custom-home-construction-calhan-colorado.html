Why Calhan Properties Need Professional Custom Home Construction

Building a custom home in Calhan, Colorado, is an exciting venture that allows homeowners to create a unique living space tailored to their needs and tastes. With an elevation of over 8,000 feet, this mountain community presents particular challenges that require the expertise of a licensed and insured general contractor like Tri-Lakes Contracting. From navigating the complex local regulations to dealing with unique soil conditions and weather patterns, having a professional custom home builder can make all the difference. Our experience ensures that we can manage the nuances of home construction in this area, helping you avoid costly pitfalls while maximizing the potential of your property.

Local Challenges in El Paso County

Calhan's rugged mountain terrain comes with its own set of challenges for home construction. The region is characterized by rocky granite, decomposed granite, and clay soils, which can complicate foundation engineering. Additionally, many properties sit on steep slopes, making access to the site during winter months difficult. The local water table is often deep, requiring homeowners to install cisterns or rely on water hauling, which can add to the overall project costs. Furthermore, with a frost line that extends 36 to 48 inches, it is crucial to have a solid understanding of local building permits and requirements, especially concerning fire mitigation strategies in forested areas. Understanding these challenges can help ensure a smoother construction process.

Our Custom Home Construction Process

At Tri-Lakes Contracting, we follow a detailed and methodical process for custom home construction that ensures every aspect is covered. First and foremost, we recommend a Build Feasibility Report before purchasing land. This report assesses soil conditions, elevation, and utility availability, helping you make an informed decision. Once the property is secured, we move to obtain your well or cistern permit, which is vital for securing a building permit.

Next, we create a professional site plan that showcases the layout of your home, utilities, and septic systems, ensuring compliance with local regulations. Following this, our team works on architectural plans, which can be custom-designed or selected from our network, including resources from ArchitecturalDesigns.com. Afterward, we focus on engineered foundation and framing plans, ensuring they are tailored to withstand the local weather conditions.

Once we compile all necessary documents, including septic system plans and land surveys, we submit your application to the El Paso County Building Department. After obtaining the building permit, we can begin construction, which typically involves site preparation, foundation work, and various inspections at each stage. Our goal is to complete your new home construction efficiently while ensuring it meets all safety and design standards.

Cost Factors for Custom Home Construction in Calhan

The cost to build a custom home in Calhan can vary significantly based on several factors. On average, the cost per square foot typically ranges from $350 to $1,000+, depending on the quality of materials and finishes chosen. For example, a basic build could cost between $350 and $450 per square foot, while a luxury custom build may range from $700 to $1,000+ per square foot. For a typical 2,500 sq ft home, you can expect costs ranging from $875,000 to $1,375,000 or higher for luxury finishes.

Additionally, there are critical factors that can increase costs, such as challenging terrain and access to your property, which can affect excavation and site preparation expenses. Frequently Asked Questions About Custom Home Construction

Q: How much does it cost to build a custom home in Colorado?

A: The cost to build a custom home in Colorado can vary widely. On average, you can expect to pay between $350 and $1,000+ per square foot. For instance, a typical 2,500 sq ft home may range from $875,000 to $1,375,000, depending on materials and complexity.

Q: How long does it take to build a house?

A: The timeline for building a custom home can range from 10 to 18 months, with pre-construction planning taking an additional 2 to 6 months. Factors such as weather, site conditions, and the complexity of the project can extend this timeline.

Q: What should I look for in a custom home builder?

A: When choosing a custom home builder, look for experience in mountain construction, the ability to provide a detailed project timeline, and a solid understanding of local building codes and permits. It's also essential to review their portfolio and client testimonials.

Q: Should I get a feasibility study before buying land?

A: Yes, obtaining a feasibility study is crucial before purchasing land. It assesses factors such as soil conditions, site accessibility, and utility availability, which can save you from investing in a property that may be challenging or impossible to build on.
