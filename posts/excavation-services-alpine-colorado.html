Cost Factors for Excavation Services in Alpine

When planning your excavation project in Alpine, it‚Äôs essential to have a clear understanding of the costs involved. Here are some typical expenses you can anticipate:

  • Site Preparation/Clearing: Basic lot clearing ranges from $3,000 to $8,000 per acre, while heavy brush or tree removal can cost between $5,000 and $15,000 per acre.
  • Driveway Construction: A gravel driveway can cost between $15 and $35 per linear foot, with a typical 200ft driveway costing $5,000 to $10,000.
  • Foundation Excavation: For a standard basement excavation of about 1,500 sq. ft., expect to pay between $15,000 and $30,000. Additional costs arise for rocky conditions, potentially adding 50-200% to your overall expenses.
  • Cistern Installation: The size is determined based on the number of bedrooms (2 people per bedroom x 100 gallons/day), including excavation, tank, pumps, and trenching.
  • Utility Trenching: Standard trenching costs between $8 and $15 per linear foot, but this can increase to $20-$50 per linear foot in rocky conditions.

Given the challenging terrain, costs may increase significantly due to rocky soils, steep access, and the need for specialized equipment. Overall, basic site prep costs typically start around $8,000 and can exceed $20,000 when integrating other services.

Frequently Asked Questions About Excavation Services

Q: How much does excavation cost per cubic yard?

A: Excavation costs can vary significantly based on site conditions, but you can expect to pay around $15 to $30 per cubic yard for standard conditions. However, if rocky terrain is involved, these costs can increase considerably, reaching upwards of $50 per cubic yard.

Q: How long does site preparation take?

A: The duration for site preparation varies depending on the size of the lot and specific site challenges. Generally, for a standard residential lot, preparation could take anywhere from one to three weeks, factoring in weather conditions and equipment availability.

Q: What equipment is needed for mountain excavation?

A: Mountain excavation often requires specialized equipment such as excavators, bobcats, and backhoes to manage rocky terrain and steep slopes. These machines are essential for effective land clearing, grading, and utility trenching.

Q: Do I need permits for excavation?

A: Yes, permits are required for excavation projects in Chaffee County. All permits must go through the local Building Department to ensure compliance with zoning laws and safety regulations.
