Cost Factors for Excavation Services in Goldfield

Understanding the costs associated with excavation services in Goldfield is crucial for budgeting your project. Here are some key cost factors:

  • Site Preparation/Clearing: Basic lot clearing ranges from $3,000 to $8,000 per acre. For more extensive work, such as heavy brush removal, expect costs between $5,000 and $15,000 per acre.
  • Driveway Construction: The cost for a gravel driveway can vary significantly. For a typical 200-foot driveway, you might spend between $5,000 and $10,000, while a 500-foot driveway can range from $12,000 to $25,000.
  • Foundation Excavation: If you're considering a basement dig for a typical 1,500 sq ft home, anticipate costs between $15,000 and $30,000, especially if rock excavation is needed, which can increase costs by 50-200%.
  • Cistern Installation: For properties requiring a cistern, the size is typically based on the formula: (Bedroom Count x 2 people x 100 gallons/day). Total installation costs can vary significantly based on site conditions.
  • Utility Trenching: Standard trenching costs around $8-$15 per linear foot, but can rise to $20-$50 per linear foot in rocky conditions.

Overall, basic site preparation can start from $8,000 and go up to $20,000, while full site development could range from $40,000 to over $100,000. These costs will vary based on site conditions, access challenges, and the need for additional dirt work.

Frequently Asked Questions About Excavation Services

Q: How much does excavation cost per cubic yard?

A: The cost for excavation typically ranges between $50 and $150 per cubic yard, depending on the complexity of the soil conditions and the equipment required for the job.

Q: How long does site preparation take?

A: Site preparation can take anywhere from a few days to several weeks, depending on the size of the area, terrain challenges, and the scope of work needed.

Q: What equipment is needed for mountain excavation?

A: Mountain excavation often requires specialized equipment such as bobcats, backhoes, and excavators, particularly for challenging rocky terrain and steep slopes.

Q: Do I need permits for excavation?

A: Yes, permits are required for excavation work in Goldfield. All permits must be obtained through the Teller County Building Department to ensure compliance with local regulations.
