Why Rockvale Properties Need Professional Excavation Services

Living in the stunning mountain community of Rockvale, Colorado, presents unique opportunities and challenges for property owners. With an elevation of over 8,000 feet, the terrain here is often rugged, demanding precise and professional excavation services. As a licensed and insured excavation contractor, Tri-Lakes Contracting understands these challenges and provides expert services tailored to the specific needs of our clients.

Professional excavation ensures that your property is prepared correctly for construction or landscaping. Failing to account for the unique Rocky Mountain soil conditions can lead to costly mistakes, and only experienced contractors have the knowledge to navigate these complexities. From site preparation to land clearing, our team guarantees that your project starts off on the right foot.

Local Challenges in Fremont County

Excavating in Rockvale comes with its own set of challenges. The rocky granite and clay soils present in the area can complicate foundation excavation efforts and increase costs significantly. The frost line, ranging from 36 to 48 inches, means that excavation must go deeper to ensure proper installation of foundations and utility lines.

Furthermore, the weather can be unpredictable, with extreme temperature swings, heavy snow, and afternoon thunderstorms posing risks to excavation projects. Accessing many properties can be difficult due to steep driveways and limited winter access. Moreover, properties often require engineered septic systems due to challenging soil conditions, complicating the excavation process even further.

Our Excavation Services Process

At Tri-Lakes Contracting, we follow a thorough process to ensure quality and efficiency in our excavation services:

  1. Consultation: We start by discussing your project needs, assessing the site, and understanding specific requirements such as driveway construction or foundation excavation.
  2. Site Assessment: Our team conducts a detailed evaluation of the land, taking into account soil type, water table depth, and access challenges.
  3. Planning and Permitting: We will help navigate the Fremont County Building Department's permitting process, ensuring all necessary approvals are secured before work begins.
  4. Excavation Work: Utilizing advanced equipment like backhoes, bobcats, and dump trucks, we carry out the excavation, adhering to safety and efficiency protocols while keeping the site clean and organized.
  5. Final Grading and Cleanup: After excavation, we conduct final grading to ensure proper drainage and prepare the site for any subsequent construction.

Typically, excavation timelines can vary based on site conditions, but most projects can be completed within a few weeks, depending on the complexity and size of the job.

Cost Factors for Excavation Services in Rockvale

The cost of excavation services in Rockvale can vary significantly based on several factors. Basic site preparation and clearing typically starts from $8,000 and can go up to $20,000. For more complex projects, including driveway construction and full site development, the costs can range from $20,000 to over $100,000, particularly if rocky conditions or steep terrain are involved.

For instance, a standard foundation excavation for a basement of approximately 1,500 square feet can cost between $15,000 and $30,000, while a crawl space could range from $5,000 to $12,000. Remember that if rock excavation is needed, it can increase costs by 50-200%. Cistern installation, necessary for many properties, is based on the bedroom count; for example, if you have three bedrooms, you would need a tank capable of storing approximately 600 gallons (3 bedrooms x 2 people x 100 gallons/day).

It's essential to consider that costs may increase due to site-specific challenges such as access difficulties and the need for specialized equipment. As experienced excavation contractors, we always quote the high end of price ranges to provide transparency and avoid misunderstandings.

Frequently Asked Questions About Excavation Services

Q: How much does excavation cost per cubic yard?

A: The cost of excavation typically ranges from $15 to $50 per cubic yard, depending on factors like soil type and site accessibility. In more challenging terrains like those found in Rockvale, costs can be on the higher end due to rocky conditions.

Q: How long does site preparation take?

A: Site preparation time can vary widely based on the complexity of the project. Generally, simple site clearing can be completed within a week, while more complex excavations with grading and trenching may take several weeks.

Q: What equipment is needed for mountain excavation?

A: Mountain excavation often requires specialized equipment such as backhoes, bobcats, and excavators, particularly for rocky terrain. Our team is equipped with the necessary machinery to handle the challenges of high-elevation, mountainous sites.

Q: Do I need permits for excavation?

A: Yes, permits are typically required for excavation work in Rockvale. The Fremont County Building Department oversees the permitting process, ensuring that all work adheres to local regulations and safety standards.
