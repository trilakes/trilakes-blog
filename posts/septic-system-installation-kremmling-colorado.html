Most properties require engineered solutions due to the poor drainage capabilities of these soils. Our Septic System Installation Process

At Tri-Lakes Contracting, we follow a comprehensive process for septic installation to ensure quality and compliance. The initial phase involves performing soil testing rather than traditional perc tests. We bring a mini excavator to dig two test holes, each eight feet deep, collecting soil samples at various depths for analysis by a licensed engineer. This is crucial, as the resulting soils report dictates the type of septic system needed.

Once we have the soils report, we work on obtaining the necessary permits from the Grand County Building Department, which typically costs between $500 and $2,000. After securing approvals, we proceed with excavation, installation of the septic tank, and connecting the leach field or drain field. The entire installation process usually takes 2-4 weeks, depending on site conditions and weather. We ensure all systems are installed according to Regulation 43, which governs OWTS in Colorado, to provide long-lasting solutions that meet local standards.

Cost Factors for Septic System Installation in Kremmling

The cost of septic installation in Kremmling varies widely based on site conditions. Generally, the total installed costs for a basic engineered system start from $35,000 and can reach up to $50,000. If your property requires a sand filter system, expect to pay between $45,000 and $65,000, while advanced treatment units (ATUs) can range from $55,000 to $80,000+. Factors that can increase costs include rocky terrain, steep access, and the need for specialized engineering solutions.

Additionally, if you're considering a cistern, size your system based on bedroom count‚Äîcalculating roughly 100 gallons per day per bedroom (2 people per bedroom). Frequently Asked Questions About Septic System Installation

Q: How much does septic installation cost in Colorado?

A: The cost for septic installation in Colorado varies significantly based on site conditions. Generally, a basic engineered system starts around $35,000 and can go up to $50,000. More complex systems, like sand filters, range from $45,000 to $65,000, while advanced treatment units can exceed $55,000, depending on the specific needs of the property.

Q: How long does septic installation take?

A: Typically, the installation process for a septic system in Kremmling can take 2-4 weeks. This timeline includes obtaining necessary permits, performing soil tests, and completing the installation itself, which can be influenced by weather conditions and site accessibility.

Q: Do I need an engineered septic system?

A: In most cases, properties in Kremmling require engineered septic systems due to the challenging soil conditions and deep water table. These systems are specifically designed to function effectively in less-than-ideal environments, ensuring compliance with Colorado's Regulation 43.

Q: What is a perc test?

A: In Colorado, traditional perc tests are replaced by soil tests that provide a more accurate assessment of soil conditions. This involves digging deep test holes to analyze soil composition and drainage capabilities, which is essential for determining the right septic system for your property.
