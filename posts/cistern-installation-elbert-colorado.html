Local Challenges in El Paso County

Elbert‚Äôs unique geography and climate present distinct challenges when it comes to water storage tank installation. Cost Factors for Cistern Installation in Elbert

When considering water storage tank installation in Elbert, it‚Äôs important to understand the various cost factors involved. The total installation price can vary significantly based on site conditions, with rocky terrain often adding 20-40% to excavation costs. As a rule of thumb, you should expect costs to fall on the higher end of the spectrum due to these challenges.

For sizing your potable water storage tank, we recommend calculating based on your home‚Äôs bedroom count. Each bedroom typically accounts for two people, using about 100 gallons of water per person daily. For example:

  • A 2-bedroom home (4 people) would require 400 gallons/day.
  • A 3-bedroom home (6 people) would require 600 gallons/day.
  • A 4-bedroom home (8 people) would require 800 gallons/day.

Depending on how often you wish to refill your tank, the total capacity will vary. For instance, a 3-bedroom home needing a refill every month would require at least 18,000 gallons. The costs for the tanks themselves vary, with 5,000-gallon Norwesco tanks priced between $13,000-$15,000 and 10,000-gallon tanks ranging from $22,000-$28,000, not including installation costs.

Including excavation, pump systems, and additional components, the total installed system ranges from:

  • 5,000 gallons: $25,000-$35,000
  • 10,000 gallons: $40,000-$55,000
  • Dual tank systems (15,000-20,000 gallons): $50,000-$75,000+
  • Large capacity systems (30,000+ gallons): $75,000-$120,000+

Investing in a larger tank can lead to fewer deliveries, ultimately saving you money in the long run. Always remember that the specifics of your site can heavily influence these costs.

Frequently Asked Questions About Cistern Installation

Q: How much does a cistern cost in Colorado?

A: The cost of a cistern in Colorado typically ranges from $25,000 to $120,000, depending on the size and specific site conditions. For instance, a 5,000-gallon system may cost between $25,000-$35,000, while larger systems can exceed $75,000.

Q: What size underground water storage tank do I need?

A: The size of your underground water storage tank should be based on your home's bedroom count. For safety, assume 100 gallons per person per day. For example, a 3-bedroom home (6 people) would need a minimum of 18,000 gallons for a monthly refill schedule.

Q: How long does cistern installation take?

A: Cistern installation can take a few weeks, depending on site conditions and weather. Our team works efficiently to complete the installation with minimal disruption to your daily life.

Q: Cistern vs well - which is better?

A: Choosing between a cistern and a well depends on your property‚Äôs water table and soil conditions. Many Elbert homeowners find cisterns to be more reliable, especially when water tables are deep or wells are impractical.
