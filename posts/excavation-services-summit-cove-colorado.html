Frequently Asked Questions About Excavation Services

Q: How much does excavation cost per cubic yard?

A: The cost of excavation typically ranges from $50 to $150 per cubic yard, depending on factors such as soil type and the complexity of the work involved. For rocky or difficult terrain, costs can increase significantly due to the additional labor and equipment required.

Q: How long does site preparation take?

A: Site preparation usually takes between one to three weeks, depending on the scale of the project, weather conditions, and site accessibility. Complex projects that involve heavy rock removal or extensive grading may take longer.

Q: What equipment is needed for mountain excavation?

A: Mountain excavation often requires specialized equipment such as backhoes, excavators, bobcats for tight areas, and dump trucks for material hauling. The type of equipment needed can vary based on the specific challenges of your site.

Q: Do I need permits for excavation?

A: Yes, you will need to obtain the appropriate permits for excavation from the Summit County Building Department. The requirements can vary based on the scope of your project and local regulations, so it‚Äôs essential to consult with your excavation contractor for guidance.
