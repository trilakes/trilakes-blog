Local Challenges in Douglas County

Roxborough Park presents a variety of challenges for homeowners considering cistern installation. The rocky granite and clay soil can significantly affect excavation and installation processes, often requiring engineered solutions to manage water effectively. The frost line here typically extends 36 to 48 inches deep, meaning that any buried water tank must be installed below this level to avoid damage from freezing temperatures. Additionally, extreme weather conditions, including heavy snowfall and afternoon thunderstorms, necessitate robust systems designed for durability.

Accessing some properties can be complicated due to steep driveways and limited winter access, which can hinder timely refills of your cistern. Many homeowners are turning to water hauling as a solution, but this can be costly and impractical during the winter months. By installing a properly sized water storage tank, you can ensure a consistent supply of water throughout the year, especially in remote or off-grid situations. Understanding these local challenges is crucial when planning your water supply system.

Our Cistern Installation Process

The cistern installation process begins with a thorough consultation to assess your unique needs and property conditions. We evaluate your daily water usage, which is generally calculated at 100 gallons per person per day, and factor in the number of bedrooms in your home to determine the appropriate tank size. For instance, a three-bedroom house with six residents would require a minimum of 600 gallons per day.

Next, we prepare to excavate the site, which involves assessing soil conditions and accessibility. Excavation costs can range from $3,000 to $8,000+, depending on the complexity of the ground and access challenges. Once the site is ready, we will install the underground water storage tank, typically a high-quality Norwesco tank known for its durability and reliability. This phase may take several days to complete, including the installation of the necessary pump system, pressure tank, and any required electrical hookups.

After installation, we ensure that your system is properly winterized, particularly important given Roxborough Park's harsh winters. This may involve implementing freeze protection measures to maintain the integrity of your potable water storage. Overall, our process is designed to be thorough and efficient, ensuring you have reliable access to water all year round.

Cost Factors for Cistern Installation in Roxborough Park

When considering water storage tank installation in Roxborough Park, it's essential to understand the various cost factors involved. The total installed costs for a basic 5,000-gallon system typically range from $25,000 to $35,000, while a 10,000-gallon system can range from $40,000 to $55,000. These estimates include the tank, excavation, pump systems, trenching to your home, and necessary electrical work.

The size of your underground cistern will heavily depend on your daily water usage and how frequently you wish to refill your system. For example, if your family uses 600 gallons a day and you want to refill every three months, a tank size between 36,000 and 54,000 gallons is recommended.
