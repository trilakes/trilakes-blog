Cost Factors for Cistern Installation in Falcon

The total cost of water storage tank installation in Falcon varies based on several factors, including the type and size of the cistern, excavation costs, and additional components like pumps and electrical setup. For example, a 5,000-gallon Norwesco tank typically costs between $13,000 and $15,000, while a 10,000-gallon tank can range from $22,000 to $28,000. To determine the size of your cistern, use this formula: number of bedrooms x 2 people per bedroom x 100 gallons per person per day.
