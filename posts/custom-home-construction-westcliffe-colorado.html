Why Westcliffe Properties Need Professional Custom Home Construction

Building a custom home in Westcliffe, Colorado, is an exciting venture that holds great promise, especially with the stunning mountain views and serene atmosphere. However, constructing a home at an elevation of over 8,000 feet presents unique challenges that require the expertise of a professional custom home builder. The harsh winters and unpredictable weather patterns demand meticulous planning and design. Whether it's navigating through Colorado's building permits or understanding the local terrain's impact on foundation engineering, having a licensed and insured general contractor like Tri-Lakes Contracting ensures that your dream home is built efficiently and safely.

Local Challenges in Custer County

Westcliffe's mountainous terrain, characterized by rocky granite and clay in the valleys, poses significant challenges for home construction. The water table is often deep, requiring many properties to utilize cisterns for water supply, which adds complexity to site planning. Additionally, winter access can be limited due to steep driveways and heavy snowfall, making it essential to prepare for winterizing custom home construction well in advance. Our Custom Home Construction Process

At Tri-Lakes Contracting, we follow a comprehensive custom home construction process that ensures your project is executed smoothly from start to finish. The first step is obtaining a Build Feasibility Report, which involves a thorough site visit, soil analysis, and water flow assessment. This critical assessment helps determine if your land is suitable for building before you make any commitments.

Once you've secured the land and completed the feasibility study, the next step involves applying for a well permit if you'll be drilling a well or planning for a cistern. This application should be filed early, as the wait times for well drilling can be extensive in our region. After that, we create a detailed site plan that outlines the home‚Äôs footprint, utility routes, and drainage patterns, ensuring compliance with local building codes.

Next, we develop architectural plans tailored to your vision, whether you choose to work with your architect or utilize our network of professionals, including options available at ArchitecturalDesigns.com. Once the plans are finalized, we will provide engineered foundation and framing plans, ensuring they meet the stringent requirements for mountain construction.

The construction phase involves detailed scheduling, regular inspections, and close communication with you to ensure your vision comes to life. Finally, upon passing the final inspection, we will obtain the certificate of occupancy, allowing you to move into your dream home.

Cost Factors for Custom Home Construction in Westcliffe

When considering a custom home build in Westcliffe, understanding the costs associated with new construction is essential. The cost per square foot for basic builds typically starts at around $350, reaching up to $450, while standard quality homes can range from $450 to $550 per square foot. For more luxurious homes, the costs can escalate to $700 and beyond, depending on design choices and materials used.

It's important to note that additional elements, such as cisterns and septic systems, must also be factored into the overall budget. For instance, a cistern can range from $25,000 to $75,000 depending on its size, which is generally calculated as 2 people per bedroom times 100 gallons per day. This means if you're planning a three-bedroom home, expect your cistern costs to start at approximately $15,000.

Other factors that significantly influence costs include the complexity of your site conditions‚Äîrocky and difficult terrains will increase excavation and foundation costs. As materials fluctuate and winter weather can delay construction timelines, it‚Äôs wise to budget for the higher end of the spectrum to accommodate these potential challenges.

Frequently Asked Questions About Custom Home Construction

Q: How much does it cost to build a custom home in Colorado?

A: The cost to build a custom home in Colorado varies widely based on factors such as location and design. In Westcliffe, you can expect to pay between $350 to $1,000+ per square foot, depending on the complexity and luxury level of your build.

Q: How long does it take to build a house?

A: Typically, the entire process from pre-construction to move-in can take anywhere from 10 to 18 months, with pre-construction planning potentially taking an additional 2 to 6 months. Weather can further extend this timeline, especially at higher elevations.

Q: What should I look for in a custom home builder?

A: When choosing a custom home builder, it‚Äôs crucial to look for experience in mountain construction, strong communication skills, and a robust portfolio of successful projects. Ensure they are licensed and insured, and ask for references to gauge their reliability and quality of work.

Q: Should I get a feasibility study before buying land?

A: Absolutely! A feasibility study helps identify potential issues with the land before purchase, including zoning restrictions, soil conditions, and water access. This report can save you from making a costly mistake and ensure your property is suitable for building.
