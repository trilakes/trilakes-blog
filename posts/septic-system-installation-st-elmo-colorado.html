About St. Elmo

St. Elmo is a historic ghost town located in Chaffee County, Colorado. Established in the 1870s during the Colorado Gold Rush, this charming locale is surrounded by stunning mountain vistas and natural beauty. Many people are drawn to St. Elmo for its rich history, outdoor recreational opportunities, and peaceful living. Hiking, fishing, and exploring the nearby forests are just a few of the activities that attract nature enthusiasts to the area. The small community fosters a sense of belonging, making it an ideal place for those looking to escape the hustle and bustle of city life.
