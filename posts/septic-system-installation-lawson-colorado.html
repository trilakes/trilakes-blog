Local Challenges in Clear Creek County

The unique characteristics of Lawson‚Äôs landscape present several challenges for septic installation. Our Septic System Installation Process

At Tri-Lakes Contracting, our approach to septic installation is comprehensive and tailored to the specific needs of your property. Here‚Äôs a step-by-step overview of our septic installation process:

  1. Initial Consultation: We start with a consultation to understand your property‚Äôs requirements and any specific concerns you may have.
  2. Soil Testing: We conduct essential soil testing using our mini excavator to dig two test holes and collect samples for a licensed engineer to assess. This helps us determine the best type of septic system to install.

Cost Factors for Septic System Installation in Lawson

The cost of septic installation in Lawson can vary significantly based on various factors, including site conditions, accessibility, and soil type. In general, the total installed costs for an engineered system can range from $35,000 to $50,000, while more complex systems, such as sand filter systems, can cost between $45,000 and $65,000. Advanced Treatment Units (ATUs) may run from $55,000 to $80,000+, especially if your property‚Äôs challenges require specialized solutions.

When calculating costs for cisterns, remember the formula: bedroom count x 2 people x 100 gallons/day. For example, a three-bedroom home would need a cistern capable of handling 600 gallons per day.

Additionally, factors that can increase costs include rocky terrain, limited access, and the requirement for deeper installations due to the frost line. Always expect to quote on the higher end of ranges due to these variables.

Q: How much does septic installation cost in Colorado?

Q: How long does septic installation take?
