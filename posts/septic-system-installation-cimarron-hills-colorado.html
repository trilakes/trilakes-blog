Local Challenges in El Paso County

Cimarron Hills is known for its stunning landscapes, but this beauty often comes with significant challenges for homeowners, particularly regarding septic system installation. The rocky granite and clay soils can make traditional systems impractical, necessitating engineered solutions that comply with local regulations. The water table is often quite deep, which may require the installation of cisterns for water supply, further complicating the septic design.

Additionally, the frost line in this region can reach depths of 36-48 inches, meaning systems must be installed well below this level to prevent freezing, especially during the harsh Colorado winters. Cost Factors for Septic System Installation in Cimarron Hills

The cost of septic installation in Cimarron Hills can vary significantly based on various factors. For a typical engineered system, you can expect to pay between $35,000 and $50,000. If your property requires a sand filter system, the total installed cost can range from $45,000 to $65,000, while an Advanced Treatment Unit (ATU) may set you back between $55,000 and $80,000+. These prices reflect the need for specialized systems that comply with Regulation 43 due to the challenging soil conditions in the area.

When planning for a septic system, it‚Äôs essential to consider additional factors that may increase costs. Rocky terrain can add $10,000 to $25,000 to the installation price, while the need for enhanced excavation due to deep frost lines will also contribute to higher expenses. If your property requires a cistern, the size is typically calculated based on bedroom count, estimating 100 gallons per day for two occupants per bedroom.

Permits from the El Paso County Building Department can range from $500 to $2,000, and soils testing and engineering typically costs between $2,000 and $4,000. Understanding these cost factors can help you budget effectively for your septic installation.

Frequently Asked Questions About Septic System Installation

Q: How much does septic installation cost in Colorado?

A: The cost of septic installation in Colorado can vary widely. For most properties in Cimarron Hills, you can expect to pay between $35,000 and $50,000 for a basic engineered system. More complex systems, like sand filters or ATUs, can range from $45,000 to $80,000+ depending on site conditions.

Q: How long does septic installation take?

A: The timeline for septic installation can vary based on factors such as permitting, weather, and site conditions. Typically, the installation process can take anywhere from a few days to a couple of weeks once permits have been secured and environmental conditions allow for excavation.

Q: Do I need an engineered septic system?

A: Most properties in Cimarron Hills require an engineered septic system due to challenging soils and local regulations. These systems are designed to effectively manage wastewater in areas where traditional systems would fail, ensuring compliance with Regulation 43.

Q: What is a perc test?

A: In Colorado, we perform soils tests instead of traditional perc tests. This involves digging test holes and analyzing soil samples to determine the best type of septic system for your property, ensuring that the system will function properly based on local soil conditions.
