Cost Factors for Excavation Services in Granby

Understanding the potential costs associated with excavation services in Granby is essential for budgeting your project. Here are some typical price ranges:

  • Site Preparation/Clearing: Basic lot clearing starts at $8,000, while heavy brush removal can reach $15,000 per acre. Stump grinding costs between $150 and $500 per stump.
  • Driveway Construction: A gravel driveway costs about $25,000 for a steep or switchback design, with typical costs ranging from $15 to $35 per linear foot.
  • Foundation Excavation: A typical basement dig for a 1,500 sq ft area runs between $15,000 and $30,000, while crawl spaces are generally $5,000 to $12,000.
  • Utility Trenching: Standard trenching is approximately $8 to $15 per linear foot, but rocky conditions can raise this to as much as $50 per linear foot.
For cistern sizing, remember: 2 people per bedroom x 100 gallons/day for water needs. Ensure you factor in the full installation costs, including excavation and trenching.

Keep in mind that costs can vary significantly depending on site conditions, such as rocky terrain, access issues, and the necessity of engineered septic systems. Our team at Tri-Lakes Contracting will provide a detailed estimate tailored to your specific project needs.

Frequently Asked Questions About Excavation Services

Q: How much does excavation cost per cubic yard?

A: Excavation costs typically start from $50 to $150 per cubic yard, depending on the site conditions and complexity of the project. Rocky terrain or challenging access can significantly increase costs.

Q: How long does site preparation take?

A: Site preparation can take anywhere from a few days to a couple of weeks, depending on the size of the lot and the scope of work. Factors such as weather conditions and soil type can also affect the timeline.

Q: What equipment is needed for mountain excavation?

A: Mountain excavation typically requires specialized equipment like excavators, bobcats, and backhoes. These machines are essential for navigating rocky terrain and performing heavy lifting tasks efficiently.

Q: Do I need permits for excavation?

A: Yes, excavation projects in Granby require permits from the Grand County County building department. We handle the permitting process as part of our excavation services to ensure compliance with local regulations.
