Cimarron Hills presents unique challenges for home construction that require careful planning and skilled execution. The rocky granite and clay soils prevalent in the area can complicate foundation engineering, often necessitating engineered systems for septic installation due to stringent regulations in El Paso County. Our Custom Home Construction Process

At Tri-Lakes Contracting, we follow a comprehensive **custom home construction** process that ensures your vision becomes a reality, step by step. 1. **Pre-Purchase Build Feasibility Study**: We recommend starting with a build feasibility report to assess the viability of your property before purchase. This includes a site visit, evaluation of setback requirements, and a water flow analysis to determine effective drainage. 2. **Well Permit or Cistern Planning**: Securing a well permit is crucial in Colorado. If using a cistern, it's an excellent option to start building quickly, as it provides immediate water access. 3. **Site Planning**: A detailed site plan outlines the positioning of your home, driveway access, utility routes, and septic placement, adhering to local regulations and topographic considerations. 4. **Architectural Plans**: Whether you have an architect or wish to utilize our network or resources like ArchitecturalDesigns.com, we ensure your home's design aligns with your vision and site conditions. 5. **Engineered Foundation & Framing Plans**: Our team collaborates with licensed engineers to develop foundation and framing plans designed to endure the unique challenges of mountain weather and soil conditions. 6. **Land Survey**: A professional survey will confirm property boundaries and identify any easements or topographic features that must be considered during construction. 7. **Septic System Plans**: If you're not connected to a municipal sewer, we handle the design and installation of engineered septic systems, which is vital given the area's challenging soil conditions. 8. **Building Permit Compilation**: We compile all necessary documents for submission to the El Paso County Building Department, ensuring timely permit approval. 9. **Pre-Construction Planning**: This phase includes finalizing your plans, ordering materials, and scheduling subcontractors, all tailored to your timeline. 10. **Construction**: From site preparation to securing your **certificate of occupancy**, we manage every phase with care, ensuring inspections are passed and quality standards are met. The entire process from pre-construction to move-in can take anywhere from 10 to 18 months, depending on various factors, including site conditions and weather.

Cost Factors for Custom Home Construction in Cimarron Hills

When considering **new construction** in Cimarron Hills, understanding the financial implications is crucial. The cost per square foot typically ranges from $550 to $700 for custom builds, with luxury options soaring above $1,000 per square foot. For example, a **1,500 sq ft** home could range from $825,000 to $1,050,000, while a **3,000 sq ft** luxury home could easily exceed $1,750,000. It is essential to consider additional costs beyond the home construction. For cistern installation, homeowners should budget approximately $75,000, calculated based on the number of bedrooms (2 people per bedroom x 100 gallons/day). Furthermore, rocky terrain, steep driveways, and engineered septic systems can significantly increase your overall costs, making it wise to quote on the higher end of estimates to ensure financial readiness. Other factors impacting costs include securing building permits, which can range from $5,000 to $20,000, and the addition of utility extensions, which vary widely based on proximity to existing services.
