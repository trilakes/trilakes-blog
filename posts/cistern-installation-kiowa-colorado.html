Frequently Asked Questions About Cistern Installation

Q: How much does a cistern cost in Colorado?

A: The cost of a cistern in Colorado can vary widely based on size and site conditions. For a typical installation, a 5,000-gallon cistern can cost between $25,000 and $35,000, while a 10,000-gallon system may range from $40,000 to $55,000, depending on excavation and equipment needs.

Q: What size underground water storage tank do I need?

A: Your tank size should be determined by calculating daily water usage based on your home's bedroom count. Generally, you should plan for 100 gallons of water per person.
