Local Challenges in Fremont County

Fremont County presents unique challenges for homeowners regarding septic system installations. The region's rocky granite and clay soils can complicate standard installations, making engineered systems a necessity for most properties. Additionally, with the water table often lying deep below ground, many residents require cisterns for water storage and hauling. The frost line here reaches 36-48 inches, creating winterization challenges that must be factored into the septic installation process. Furthermore, extreme temperature swings and heavy snow loads demand robust and reliable onsite wastewater management solutions. These factors, combined with the need for fire mitigation in forested areas, highlight the importance of hiring a knowledgeable septic contractor who understands the local landscape.

Our Septic System Installation Process

At Tri-Lakes Contracting, we follow a thorough and systematic process for septic installation. Cost Factors for Septic System Installation in Rockvale

The cost of septic installation in Rockvale can vary significantly based on various factors. On average, a basic engineered system will range from $35,000 to $50,000, while a sand filter system might cost between $45,000 and $65,000. For those requiring an Advanced Treatment Unit, expect costs starting from $55,000 and potentially reaching $80,000 or more, particularly if site conditions are challenging.

When planning for a cistern, the size should be calculated based on the number of bedrooms in your home, typically estimating 2 people per bedroom at 100 gallons per day. For example, a 3-bedroom home would need a cistern capable of holding at least 600 gallons. Additional costs arise from site-specific challenges such as rocky terrain, steep access roads, and the need for more extensive excavation, which can add $10,000 to $25,000 to your total. Always remember that the total installed costs should include all components, such as the tank, excavation, pumps, trenching, and any necessary electrical systems.

Frequently Asked Questions About Septic System Installation

Q: How much does septic installation cost in Colorado?

A: The cost for septic installation in Colorado typically ranges from $35,000 to $50,000 for a basic engineered system. More advanced systems, like sand filter or ATU systems, can cost between $45,000 and $80,000 or more, depending on the specific site conditions.

Q: How long does septic installation take?

A: The installation of a septic system usually takes about 1-2 weeks once all permits are acquired. The timeline may vary based on site conditions and the complexity of the system being installed.

Q: Do I need an engineered septic system?

A: Most properties in the Colorado mountains, including Rockvale, require engineered systems due to challenging soil conditions as per Regulation 43. These systems are designed to effectively handle onsite wastewater treatment, making them crucial for many properties.

Q: What is a perc test?

A: In Colorado, traditional perc tests are replaced with soil testing methods that analyze soil composition. This involves digging test holes to assess the depth and type of soil, which helps determine the suitable septic system for your property.
