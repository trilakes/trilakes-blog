About Ridgewood

Nestled in the foothills of the Rocky Mountains, the Ridgewood neighborhood offers an enchanting blend of natural beauty and community charm just a stone's throw from Woodland Park, Colorado. Known for its breathtaking views, Ridgewood attracts those looking to escape the hustle and bustle of city life while still maintaining access to essential amenities. The area is characterized by its spacious lots, perfect for building custom homes that embrace the surrounding landscape. The community thrives on outdoor activities, making it ideal for families, retirees, and anyone looking for a quieter lifestyle. Properties here typically range from single-family homes to larger, custom-built residences, with many showcasing stunning mountain vistas and ample privacy. With the captivating backdrop of Pikes Peak and the nearby national forest, Ridgewood is a haven for nature lovers and outdoor enthusiasts alike.

Septic Considerations

Most properties in Ridgewood are not connected to a municipal sewer system, so homeowners typically utilize septic systems for wastewater management. The common types of septic systems include conventional systems, alternative systems, and engineered systems, depending on the terrain and soil conditions. Given the mountainous landscape, the terrain can vary significantly, influencing septic system design and installation.
