About Fourmile Area

The Fourmile Area, located just off the scenic Highway 285 near Fairplay, Colorado, is a charming mountain community that offers a blend of natural beauty and recreational opportunities. Nestled at an elevation of approximately 10,000 feet, the area is surrounded by stunning views of the Mosquito Range and offers easy access to outdoor activities, including fishing, hiking, and snow sports. Its close proximity to Fairplay provides residents with necessary amenities, yet the peaceful, rural setting of Fourmile allows for a quiet retreat from city life.

What makes Fourmile particularly appealing is its sense of community; residents often enjoy the tranquility of mountain living while still being part of a vibrant neighborhood. The area is characterized by a mix of single-family homes, mountain cabins, and vacant land parcels, making it suitable for both buyers looking for a primary residence and those interested in vacation homes or investment properties.

Water & Utilities

In the Fourmile Area, residents typically rely on wells or cisterns for their water supply, as there is no municipal water system available. Most properties have their own private wells, which can be a cost-effective solution for water needs. However, it's essential for prospective buyers to conduct thorough research regarding the well water quality and quantity, as this can vary significantly between properties. Resources such as ColoradoWell.com can provide valuable insights into existing wells in the area, helping you gauge the reliability of water access before making a purchase.
