About McLain Flats

McLain Flats is a serene, upscale neighborhood located just a stone's throw from the vibrant town of Aspen, Colorado. Known for its breathtaking views of the surrounding mountains and proximity to outdoor recreational activities, this area is particularly appealing to those seeking a tranquil yet luxurious lifestyle. With its blend of spacious lots and stunning homes, McLain Flats offers a unique combination of privacy and convenience. Property types in this neighborhood range from contemporary mountain retreats to classic alpine lodges, catering to a diverse clientele including families, retirees, and second-home buyers. The area's well-maintained roads and proximity to both nature and amenities make it a desirable place to build or buy a home.

Water & Utilities

In McLain Flats, homeowners typically rely on wells for their water supply, as the area is not connected to municipal water services. It is essential to understand the water infrastructure before making a purchase or building a home in this neighborhood. Wells can vary in depth and yield, so conducting thorough research is crucial. A good resource for potential well owners is ColoradoWell.com, which provides valuable information about well regulations and best practices. In addition, you may need to consider water storage solutions, such as cisterns, especially for larger properties that require substantial water access. Additionally, electricity and gas services are readily available, making it easier to connect utilities to your new home.

Building & Permits

Building and permitting in McLain Flats falls under the jurisdiction of Pitkin County, which has specific regulations governing construction in this area. Before starting any building project, it is essential to familiarize yourself with the local zoning laws and building codes.
