About Four Mile Ranch

Four Mile Ranch is a picturesque neighborhood located just a few miles from the heart of Canon City, Colorado. Nestled among the stunning backdrop of the Rocky Mountains, this community offers a unique blend of rural charm and modern conveniences. The area is known for its tranquil atmosphere, making it an appealing choice for families, retirees, and outdoor enthusiasts alike. With a variety of property types available, from spacious single-family homes to expansive lots ripe for custom builds, Four Mile Ranch caters to diverse preferences and lifestyles. The neighborhood is well-situated near essential amenities, including shopping, dining, and recreational activities, while still providing that desired sense of seclusion. The proximity to outdoor adventures, such as hiking, biking, and fishing, enhances its allure, making Four Mile Ranch a desirable place to call home.
