About Custer County Ranches

Nestled in the picturesque Wet Mountain Valley near Westcliffe, Colorado, Custer County Ranches offers a unique blend of rural charm and breathtaking mountain views. This neighborhood is characterized by expansive parcels of land that provide potential homeowners with the opportunity to embrace the serene Colorado lifestyle while enjoying ample space for outdoor activities. The area appeals to those seeking a tranquil retreat, with its stunning landscapes, abundant wildlife, and proximity to the Sangre de Cristo Mountains. Property types in Custer County Ranches vary widely, ranging from modest cabins and manufactured homes to sprawling custom-built estates, making it a versatile choice for a diverse range of buyers.

Septic Considerations

Most homes in Custer County Ranches utilize septic systems rather than municipal sewer systems. The type of septic system you may need will depend on the soil conditions and the size of the property. Conventional septic systems are common, but alternative systems may be required in areas with challenging terrain, such as rocky or sloped lots. Before purchasing land, it is crucial to conduct a soil test to determine the suitability of each lot for septic installation. This will ensure compliance with local health regulations and proper waste management. Consulting with local experts and engineers can provide valuable insights into the best septic solutions for your specific property.
