About Sanctuary Pointe

Sanctuary Pointe is a picturesque residential neighborhood located just south of Monument, Colorado, nestled against the stunning backdrop of the Rocky Mountains. This area is known for its serene environment, well-planned community layout, and breathtaking natural beauty, making it a highly appealing choice for families, retirees, and outdoor enthusiasts alike. The neighborhood features a mix of single-family homes, custom builds, and townhomes, catering to a variety of lifestyles and budgets. Many properties boast large lots, allowing for ample outdoor space, while the community is designed with walking paths, parks, and open spaces that promote an active, healthy lifestyle. The close-knit community feel, coupled with easy access to nearby amenities and recreational opportunities, makes Sanctuary Pointe an attractive place to call home.

Water & Utilities

Sanctuary Pointe is serviced by municipal water, ensuring that residents have reliable access to clean drinking water. Additionally, electricity, natural gas, and high-speed internet are readily available in the area, making it convenient for modern living.
